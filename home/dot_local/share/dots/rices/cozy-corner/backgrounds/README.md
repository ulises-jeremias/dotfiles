# Cozy Corner Wallpapers

Add cozy and kawaii wallpapers here. Recommended themes:

- Cozy rooms with warm lighting
- Cute animals (cats, bunnies, bears)
- Coffee/tea aesthetic scenes
- Houseplants and botanical art
- Studio Ghibli-inspired scenes
- Lofi girl aesthetic
- Soft clouds and pastel skies

Recommended colors: Soft pink (#f5c2e7), Cream (#f5e0dc), Warm brown (#7c5c4f), Sage green (#a6e3a1)
